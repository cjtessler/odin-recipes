Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish made by layering meat, cheese, and nooddles.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • ground meat
  • pasta sauce
  • cheese
  • salt and pepper to seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles.
  2. Brown meat.
  3. Add pasta sauce to cooked meat.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake.
